Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 4, 2026

The short version: Potoos is a private, invite-only app. Your files are yours. We store them securely and never share them with anyone outside the people you invite.

1. What We Collect

When you sign in with Google, we receive:

  • Your name and email address (from your Google account)
  • Your Google profile photo (used as your avatar inside the app)

When you use the app, we also store:

  • The photos and videos you upload (stored in Google Drive)
  • Album names, descriptions, and membership records
  • Upload timestamps and file metadata (filename, size, type)

We do not collect location data, device identifiers, advertising IDs, or behavioral analytics.

2. How We Store Your Files

Your uploaded photos and videos are stored in Google Drive via the Google Drive API, in a private folder that belongs to the Potoos storage account. Files are stored at full original quality — we never compress, resize, or modify your files.

Album metadata (names, members, file records) is stored in a Supabase-hosted PostgreSQL database with row-level security — only members of an album can read its data.

3. Who Can See Your Files

Potoos is invite-only. Your files and albums are only visible to:

  • You (the uploader and/or album admin)
  • Members you explicitly invite to an album

There are no public feeds, no discovery features, and no way for third parties or other Potoos users to find or access your albums.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following services to operate Potoos:

  • Google OAuth — sign-in authentication
  • Google Drive API — file storage
  • Supabase — database, authentication, and server-side functions

Each service has its own privacy policy. We recommend reviewing them if you want to understand how your data is handled at the infrastructure level.

5. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account from within the app, we permanently remove:

  • All albums you own and their files from Google Drive
  • Your memberships in all other albums
  • Your profile and authentication record

This deletion is permanent and cannot be reversed.

6. Children's Privacy

Potoos is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
