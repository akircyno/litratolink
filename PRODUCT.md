# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Potoos is used by private groups who want a simple invite-only place to upload,
view, and save shared photos and videos. Users are usually on phones, often
checking an album after someone else adds new originals.

## Product Purpose

Potoos preserves and shares original-quality media without compression. Success
means contributors can upload the real file, members can preview quickly, and
download/save flows retrieve the original asset.

## Brand Personality

Warm, careful, and trustworthy. Poto adds a gentle mascot presence, but the app
should stay focused on the user task.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic gallery clutter, hidden destructive actions, compression-first
photo tools, and dramatic redesigns that drift away from the committed mobile
HTML mockup.

## Design Principles

- Original files are sacred: previews may be lighter, downloads must stay real.
- Private means understandable: permissions, invites, and deletes should be
  clear before they change shared data.
- Mobile first, one-handed friendly: primary controls should be easy to reach
  and hard to mis-tap.
- Familiar controls beat novelty: use standard action sheets, confirmations,
  selections, and toasts.
- Failures should explain the next useful action.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Use readable contrast, large touch targets, reduced-motion friendly state
changes, and copy that explains destructive actions without relying on color
alone.
